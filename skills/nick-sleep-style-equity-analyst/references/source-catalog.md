# Source Catalog

Use this file to gather source-backed context without copying long copyrighted
material. Prefer the approved public source, link to provenance, and keep notes
as concise paraphrases.

## Source Handling Rules

- Primary material first, secondary commentary second.
- Do not bulk-copy Nomad letters, book text, transcripts, articles, or paid
  material into skill outputs.
- Use short excerpts only when they are necessary and keep them well below
  copyright limits.
- For any current stock analysis, gather fresh company and market sources in
  addition to these evergreen references.
- If a redistributed PDF conflicts with the IGY Foundation copy, prefer the IGY
  Foundation version.

  to customers, with short-term expense traded for long-term value.
- Around PDF pages 150-151: Amazon cost position, lower prices, convenience, and
  long runway from offline retail share shift.
- Around PDF pages 214-215: founder motivation, meaning in work, and inactivity
  as a deliberate source of value.

## Public Archives And Redistributed Copies

- Public PDF mirrors, Scribd uploads, personal blogs, and file-hosted copies may
  be useful for discovery but can have uncertain permissions or privacy edits.
- Do not quote or link a mirror when the same material is available from the IGY
  Foundation approved version.
- If a user supplies a redistributed copy, compare it against the IGY Foundation
  copy before using it.

## Recurring Business Examples

Use examples as analogies only after checking the current company evidence.

- Costco: low-cost culture, customer value, membership trust, long-term owner
  behavior, and scale benefits shared with customers.
- Amazon: reinvestment, lower prices, convenience, selection, Prime, logistics,
  and temporary margin suppression for long-term advantage.
- Berkshire Hathaway: owner orientation, decentralized culture, capital
  allocation, patience, and durable compounding.
- Other Nomad examples to check in the letters when relevant: AirAsia, Asos,
  Carpetright, Stagecoach, and selected financials or retailers discussed in
  specific letters.
