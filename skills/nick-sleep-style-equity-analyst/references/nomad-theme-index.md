## Theme Weighting

For a long-term compounder view, emphasize:

- Customer proposition and scale economies shared.
- Reinvestment runway and capital allocation.
- Culture and incentives.
- Balance sheet resilience.
- Valuation versus 5-10 year destination.

De-emphasize:

- One-quarter beats and misses.
- Short-term price action unless it changes prospective return.
- Management storytelling not backed by customer or financial evidence.
