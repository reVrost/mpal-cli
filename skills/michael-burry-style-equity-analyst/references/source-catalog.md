# Source Catalog

This catalog stores public reference links and paraphrased research notes for
the `michael-burry-style-equity-analyst` skill. It is not a transcript, letter,
filing, book, Substack, or article archive. Use links, metadata, and concise
notes to calibrate analysis while staying within copyright limits.

## Use Rules

- Do not copy full Scion letters, articles, transcripts, Substack posts, books,
  or filings into outputs or repo memory.
- Separate primary sources from secondary sources.
- Treat 13F holdings as incomplete, stale snapshots of certain long US-listed
  securities and reported options positions. They do not reveal whole-portfolio
  risk, cost basis, intra-quarter trades, shorts, swaps, or private holdings.
- For live analysis, fetch fresh data and recent filings instead of relying on
  this catalog.
