# Source Catalog

This catalog stores public reference links and concise, paraphrased research notes for the `warren-buffett-style-equity-analyst` skill. It is not a letter, book, transcript, or article archive.

## Use Rules

- Prefer primary sources: Berkshire Hathaway letters, annual reports, SEC filings, company filings, official annual meeting transcripts/videos, and official company materials.
- Do not bulk-copy shareholder letters, books, transcripts, or articles. Use links, metadata, and concise paraphrases.
- Treat secondary sources as calibration only. Do not cite them as proof of Buffett's views without checking the underlying primary source.
- For current stock analysis, fetch fresh company filings, prices, valuation data, earnings releases, transcripts, and credible recent news.
- Do not claim the output is Buffett's actual current view or private view.

## Primary Sources

### Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters

URL: https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/letters/letters.html

Notes:

- Official Berkshire archive for annual shareholder letters.
- Use for recurring discussions of intrinsic value, per-share progress, float, insurance underwriting, acquisitions, accounting, capital allocation, repurchases, risk, and owner communication.
- Use as the first source when mapping a principle to Buffett's public written record.

### Berkshire Hathaway Owner's Manual

URL: https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/owners.html

PDF mirror often linked by search: https://berkshirehathaway.com/ownman.pdf

Notes:

- Official owner-orientation document describing Berkshire's partnership mentality, long-term owner base, communication philosophy, acquisition posture, and limits.
- Use for owner-minded framing, management candor, and the idea that shareholders are partners rather than trading counterparties.

### Berkshire Hathaway Annual Reports

URL: https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/reports.html

Notes:

- Official annual report archive.
- Use for source-backed examples of operating earnings, insurance float, underwriting results, segment economics, acquisitions, cash and Treasury holdings, buybacks, debt, and Berkshire's financial statements.

### Warren Buffett Archive By CNBC

URL: https://buffett.cnbc.com/warren-buffett-archive/

Notes:

- CNBC describes the archive as a large collection of Buffett speaking about business, investing, money, and life.
- Use for annual meeting Q&A, CNBC interviews, topic clips, and synchronized transcripts where available.
- Search the archive for specific topics such as circle of competence, moats, inflation, accounting, leverage, buybacks, mistakes, technology, banking, and management.

### CNBC Annual Meetings Archive

URL: https://buffett.cnbc.com/annual-meetings/

Notes:

- CNBC annual meeting pages organize Berkshire meeting videos and transcripts by year.
- Use for topic-specific discussion and Q&A context; cite year and topic when used.

### Public Interviews And Talks

Source path:

- Prefer official CNBC archive pages, university-hosted event pages, company-hosted recordings, or direct transcript/video pages from the publisher.

Notes:

- Use public interviews and talks to calibrate topics such as business quality, circle of competence, pricing power, accounting, inflation, cash, market cycles, owner orientation, hurdle rates, and temperament.
- Record the event name, publisher, date, URL, and whether the source is a full transcript, clip, or secondary summary.
- Do not cite short social clips without locating the fuller source where practical.

### Berkshire SEC Filings

SEC company search: https://www.sec.gov/edgar/search/

Notes:

- Use Berkshire 10-Ks, 10-Qs, 8-Ks, proxy statements, and 13F filings for current factual claims.
- For other companies, use their own SEC or exchange filings before secondary summaries.

## Partnership Letter Sources

### Buffett Partnership Letters

Source status:

- Public copies circulate in investor archives and PDF compilations. Provenance and copyright status vary.
- Use only for source-discovery and theme calibration unless a credible source and date can be recorded.
- Do not reproduce full letters or long excerpts.

Suggested search terms:

- "Buffett Partnership letters 1957 1970 PDF"
- "Buffett Partnership Ltd letters archive"
- "Warren Buffett partnership letter 1962 general stock market"

Recurring themes to verify before citing:

- Partnership comparison against the Dow.
- Workouts, generals, controls.
- Avoiding market forecasts.
- Long-term measurement and downside behavior.
- Margin of safety and security selection discipline.

## Secondary Calibration Sources

Use these only after primary-source checks:

- Lawrence Cunningham, `The Essays of Warren Buffett`, for organized topic grouping.
- Alice Schroeder, `The Snowball`, for biographical context.
- Roger Lowenstein, `Buffett: The Making of an American Capitalist`, for historical context.
- University lecture summaries and investor-study notes for finding primary passages.
- Credible business press interviews where the transcript or recording is available.

## Current-Analysis Source Checklist

For any current stock view, collect:

- Latest price, market cap, enterprise value, and valuation multiples.
- Recent 10-K/annual report and 10-Q/interim report.
- Latest earnings release, presentation, and call transcript.
- Five to ten years of revenue, margins, ROIC/ROE, free cash flow, capex, debt, and shares outstanding where available.
- Recent capital allocation actions: buybacks, dividends, acquisitions, debt issuance/repayment, equity issuance.
- Management incentive disclosures and insider/ownership context.
- Relevant industry data and competitor context.
- Current news only after primary filings are checked.
